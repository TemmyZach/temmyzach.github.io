Exploratory Data Analysis and Visualization Using Matplotlib & Seaborn in Python

On this project, I performed an EDA on a Prosper Loan dataset, sourced from Udacity. Prosper Loan is a leading marketplace lending company in the United States. The company's dataset contains information on loan origination, borrower characteristics and lender activities from 2005 to 2015. My analysis gave vital insights on how every type of borrower relates differently to lender policies and the loan market as a whole. For visualization, I employed the use of Matplotlib and Seaborn. The visualization contains univariate, bivariate and multivariate charts, depending on whether the variables are quantitative or categorical.

PwC Power BI Virtual Internship Project

I performed an indepth analysis on a telecom dataset during my virtual intership with PwC on the Forage platform. The focus of the analysis was to draw insights from call center trends, customer retention, and HR metrics. I applied the use of DAX to create accurate measures and visualized data in Power BI.
